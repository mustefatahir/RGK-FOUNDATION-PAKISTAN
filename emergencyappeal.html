COVID-19 Pandemic in Pakistan

Lockdown Appeal

Covid-19 has been declared pandemic by the World Health Organization (WHO). It is widespread across the world infecting thousands people and death toll is increasing every moment. In order to avoid the spread of virus, Government has announced the Lockdown. People are forced to stay at home and keep social distance to avoid the contact of Corona Virus but parallel to this they are unable to go to their work to earn their daily living. This situation has burdened the life of a daily-wager and those who run small businesses to earn and meet their daily life expenses. Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan is providing monthly ration at the doorstep of such needy families with the help of its generous donors. So far we have delivered ration of worth rupees one billion, but still the need of time has not yet met. Your donations can help a poor family to survive in this hard time. Come forward to give Donations, Zakat & Sadqaat for the poor and needy Pakistanis and help them observe a blessed month of Ramadan at their home.
